    • About Me

    I am pursuing a Computer Science degree at New Mexico State University. During my time at NMSU I've participated in the coding competitions hosted by Google Student Developer Club where my team and I won first place. I also have gone to Pasadena, California to attend the Great Minds in STEM conference where I participated in a hackathon that involved cracking hashed passwords, and using Wireshark to sniff packets. I was also in the Google Tech Exchange Program where I took courses taught by senior software engineers and learned industry-standard tools and technologies. This past summer I traveled to Virginia to work at my first internship with the US Army DEVCOM C5ISR center where I worked on mixed reality HoloLenses. This was to improve the training of our Army soldiers allowing them to practice in a simulated environment.
